About E-Therapy

We take certified therapists (Our Therapists) and automatically match them with anonymous users who submit their concerns on the home page.

The users can chat with certified therapists using texts, a video call, or an audio call.

Our website is different to any other online therapy, because ours is free and anonymous!

Users do not need to enter their phone number to get texts or calls because the site does it all! We contact a therapist on their phone while you can chat anonymously through the site!

Just remember Don't Be Afraid To Talk!

Make sure you DO NOT use this website in an emergency situation! Here is a suicide hotline: 1-800-273-8255
